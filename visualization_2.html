Cloudiness

As shown in the graph, Cloudiness is not clearly related to Latitude. However, it's trivial that even the pattern is random, there are some cities clustered at 0% cloudiness, and some cities have above 80% of cloudiness. Clouds play multiple critical roles in the climate system. In particular, being bright objects in the visible part of the solar spectrum, they efficiently reflect light to space and thus contribute to the cooling of the planet. Cloud cover thus plays an important role in the energetic balance of the atmosphere and a variation of it is a consequence of and to the climate change expected by recent studies.
